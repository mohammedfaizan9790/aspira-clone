Details of the Perfume

The Man Company’s Blanc and Noir perfumes are crafted for the modern gentleman, offering a perfect blend of elegance and masculinity. Blanc features a light, refreshing fragrance ideal for daytime wear, while Noir delivers a bold, intense aroma suited for evenings and special occasions. Both come in sleek 100ml bottles with a minimalist design—white with gold for Blanc, and black with silver for Noir—reflecting their distinct personalities. These perfumes not only smell luxurious but also leave a lasting impression.

Vetiver Eau de Toilette by Guerlain is a timeless men's fragrance known for its fresh, earthy, and woody scent. It opens with lively citrus top notes and transitions into a refined heart of vetiver root, creating a crisp and sophisticated aroma. First introduced in 1959, Vetiver remains a classic choice for men who appreciate elegance, masculinity, and tradition. Its clear glass bottle with a green label reflects the natural freshness of the scent, making it perfect for both daily wear and special occasions.

Bergamot Beauté Gentle Man Pure Parfum is a refined and modern fragrance crafted for the confident, sophisticated man. With rich bergamot top notes and deep, warm undertones, this perfume delivers a long-lasting scent that blends freshness with strength. Its sleek black-and-blue gradient bottle paired with a matching box reflects elegance and masculinity, making it a perfect gift or daily signature scent. Designed for those who appreciate bold yet balanced fragrances, Gentle Man leaves a lasting impression wherever you go.

Our Philosophy

At Scent & Style, we believe that fragrance is an extension of identity. We meticulously source the finest ingredients from around the world to create unique and captivating scents that resonate with your personal style. Our commitment is to quality, artistry, and sustainability, ensuring every bottle tells a story.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate perfumers, Scent & Style was born from a desire to bring exceptional fragrances to discerning individuals. We started in a small atelier, handcrafting each blend with precision and care. Today, we continue that tradition, combining age-old techniques with modern innovation to create scents that are both timeless and contemporary. Our journey is one of passion, dedication, and the pursuit of olfactory perfection.
